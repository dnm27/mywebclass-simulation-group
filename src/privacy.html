Privacy Policy

At mywebclass.org, safeguarding your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect from you when you visit our website, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using our website, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information we collect

We may collect the following information from you when you visit our website:

  • Personal information like your name and email address, which you may choose to provide us with.
  • Details about your website usage, such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and links clicked.
  • Information about your device, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect from you for the following purposes:

  • To enhance our website and the user experience.
  • To examine user behavior and evaluate the effectiveness of our content and marketing campaigns.
  • To communicate with you about our products and services, if you have shared your contact details with us.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect data about your usage of our website. Google Analytics gathers information like how frequently users visit the site, which pages they access, and the other websites they accessed before arriving at our website. We use this data to enhance our website and the user experience. Google Analytics only collects your IP address on the date you visited our website and not your name or other identifying information. We do not link the information collected via Google Analytics with personally identifiable information. Google Analytics puts a permanent cookie on your browser to recognize you as a unique user when you visit our website the next time. This cookie cannot be used by anyone else except Google. Google's ability to utilize and share information collected by Google Analytics about your website visits is governed by the Google Analytics Terms of Use and the Google Privacy Policy.

Data retention and security

We hold the information we collect for as long as it is necessary to meet the purposes for which we collected it, unless the law requires us to retain it for a longer period. We take sensible measures to safeguard the information we collect from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, and erase your personal information. You also have the right to object to the processing of your personal information and to withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at [contact email address].

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any updates by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We suggest that you check this Privacy Policy occasionally for any changes.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [contact email address].
